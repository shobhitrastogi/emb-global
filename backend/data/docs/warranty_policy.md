# Northwind Gadgets — Warranty Policy

## 1. Coverage Period
All electronic products sold by Northwind Gadgets carry a 12-month limited warranty from the date of
delivery. Accessories such as cables and cushions carry a 6-month warranty.

## 2. What Is Covered
The warranty covers manufacturing defects, including hardware failure under normal use. It does not
cover accidental damage, liquid damage, or wear and tear from misuse.

## 3. Raising a Claim
To raise a warranty claim, the customer must supply the order number and a description of the fault.
Original packaging is not required for warranty claims. Claims can be raised at any time within the
coverage period, independent of the 30-day return window.

## 4. Resolution
Valid claims are resolved by repair, replacement, or refund at the company's discretion. Replacement
units carry the remaining balance of the original warranty period, not a new 12-month term.
