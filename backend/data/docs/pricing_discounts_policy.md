# Northwind Gadgets — Pricing and Discounts Policy

## 1. Pricing
All listed prices are in Indian Rupees and are inclusive of applicable taxes. Prices may change
without notice, but the price confirmed at the time of order placement is the price honoured for that
order.

## 2. Bulk Discounts
Orders of 10 or more units of a single product qualify for a 10 percent bulk discount. Bulk discounts
must be requested before the order is placed and cannot be applied retroactively.

## 3. Promotional Codes
Only one promotional code may be applied per order. Promotional codes cannot be combined with
bulk discounts. Expired or invalid codes will be rejected at checkout.

## 4. Price Match
Northwind Gadgets does not offer price matching against third-party retailers. Refunds will not be
issued for price drops occurring after an order is placed.
