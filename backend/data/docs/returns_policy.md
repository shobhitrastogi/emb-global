# Northwind Gadgets — Returns and Refunds Policy

## 1. Return Window
Customers may return any eligible product within 30 days of the delivery date. Returns requested
after 30 days from delivery will not be accepted, except where the product is found to be defective
under the warranty terms in Section 4.

## 2. Eligibility
To be eligible for a return, the item must be unused, in its original packaging, and accompanied by
the order number. Orders with a status of 'cancelled' were never shipped and are not subject to this
policy. Orders marked 'returned' have already completed the return process.

## 3. Refund Processing
Approved refunds are issued to the original payment method within 7 to 10 business days of the
returned item being received at our warehouse. Shipping charges are non-refundable unless the
return is due to a defective or incorrect item.

## 4. Defective Items and Warranty
All electronic products carry a 12-month limited warranty against manufacturing defects. Defective
items may be returned or exchanged at any point within the warranty period, independent of the
30-day return window.

## 5. Non-Returnable Items
Software licences, gift cards, and items marked as final sale at the time of purchase cannot be
returned or refunded under any circumstances.
