# Northwind Gadgets — Product FAQ

## Shipping and Delivery
Standard delivery takes 3 to 5 business days. Orders move through the following statuses: pending,
processing, shipped, and delivered. A 'pending' order has been placed but payment confirmation is
still in progress. A 'processing' order is being prepared for dispatch from our warehouse.

## Order Tracking
Once an order reaches 'shipped' status, a tracking link is emailed to the customer. Orders that remain
in 'pending' for more than 48 hours should be raised with support, as this usually indicates a payment
issue.

## Payment Methods
We accept UPI, credit and debit cards, and net banking. Cash on delivery is available for orders
under 5,000 only. All prices on invoices are in Indian Rupees and are inclusive of applicable taxes.

## Warranty Claims
To raise a warranty claim, the customer must provide the order number and a description of the fault.
Warranty claims do not require the original packaging, unlike standard returns.

## Cancellations
An order can be cancelled by the customer only while it is in 'pending' or 'processing' status. Once an
order is 'shipped', it can no longer be cancelled and must instead be handled as a return after
delivery.
