# Northwind Gadgets — Employee Leave Policy

## 1. Annual Leave
Full-time employees are entitled to 18 days of paid annual leave per calendar year. Leave accrues at
1.5 days per completed month of service. A maximum of 6 unused leave days may be carried forward
into the following year.

## 2. Sick Leave
Employees are entitled to 12 days of paid sick leave per year. Sick leave of more than two
consecutive days requires a medical certificate.

## 3. Applying for Leave
All leave must be applied for through the HR portal at least 7 days in advance, except for sick leave,
which may be reported on the day. Leave is subject to manager approval based on team coverage.

## 4. Public Holidays
The company observes 10 public holidays each year, published in the annual HR calendar. Public
holidays are in addition to annual leave entitlement.
